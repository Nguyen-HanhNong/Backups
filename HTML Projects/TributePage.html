Who is Bob Ross?

Bob Rossm otherwise known as Robert Norman Ross was an American painter and television actor on the popular TV show, The Joy of Painting. He was born in 1942 in Daytona Beach and died in 1995. He had a hippie sortof persona and had a permed afro. He eventually was engrained in Twitch culture by becoming part of the KappaRoss emote.
